Warehouse management system

A warehouse management system (WMS) is a software application that helps manage and control the operations of a warehouse or distribution center. It can automate inventory management, order fulfillment, receiving and shipping, and provide real-time visibility into the movement and status of goods. The system can also optimize warehouse layout, labor utilization, and improve accuracy and efficiency of operations.
